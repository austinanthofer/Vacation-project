Bora Bora's white sand beaches offer luxurious relaxation for every individual or couple. With soft sand, and warm days, you can't go wrong!

The night life on the beach leads to romantic on site dinners. With the heat of the night, and light of a candle, can be any couples dream dinner.

Bora Bora is an excellent wedding location. Couples will find themeselves getting married right on the beach!

For specific information on different beach locations, and other beach events, refer to http://www.boraboraislandguide.com/bora-bora-beaches.html
